How It Works

  1. Click "Connect SumUp Account" below

    You'll be redirected to SumUp's secure login page

  2. Log in to your SumUp account (or create one)

    If you don't have a SumUp account, you can create one for free in minutes

  3. Authorize Jersey Events to create payment requests

    This allows us to send payment requests for ticket purchases directly to your account

  4. You're all set!

    Return to Jersey Events and start creating events. Customers can now buy tickets.

Important Information
  • You need a SumUp account to connect. Sign up for free if you don't have one.
  • Your SumUp merchant code will be linked to your Jersey Events account
  • You can disconnect SumUp at any time from your dashboard
  • Jersey Events only charges a £15-£70 listing fee per event - we don't take a cut of your ticket sales
