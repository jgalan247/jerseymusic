Quick Guide: Processing Refunds
On Jersey Artwork Platform:
  1. Review the refund request
  2. Communicate with the customer
  3. Approve or reject the request
  4. Document your decision
On SumUp Dashboard:
  1. Log into your SumUp account
  2. Find the transaction
  3. Process the refund
  4. Keep the receipt for records
Remember: Jersey Artwork does not process payments. All financial transactions are handled through SumUp directly between you and your customers.
