Our Story

Jersey Events was born from a simple belief: every event tells the story of our vibrant island community.

From intimate acoustic sessions in St. Helier's cozy cafes to grand festivals celebrating Jersey's rich heritage, we showcase the incredible diversity of entertainment and cultural experiences our beautiful island offers.

Our platform connects passionate event organizers with eager attendees, making it easier than ever to discover, book, and enjoy the best events Jersey has to offer. We're not just selling tickets - we're building memories and bringing people together.

To connect Jersey's vibrant community through unforgettable events and experiences, making it easier for organizers to reach their audience and for event-goers to discover amazing local experiences.

Our Vision

To be Jersey's premier event platform, celebrating our island's unique culture while fostering a thriving community where local talent and entrepreneurs can flourish.

Jersey's Rich Cultural Heritage

From ancient traditions to modern entertainment, Jersey has always been a hub of cultural activity

Jersey's event scene reflects our island's unique blend of British and French influences, seaside charm, and tight-knit community spirit. From traditional festivals celebrating our agricultural heritage to cutting-edge music events featuring international artists, our events calendar is as diverse as our landscape.

Our platform celebrates this diversity - from intimate poetry readings in St. Helier's hidden corners to large-scale festivals on our beautiful beaches, from business conferences in modern venues to traditional folk festivals in our historic parishes.
